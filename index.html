Hi, I'm Brooks!

From a young age, I’ve had a fascination with computers, starting with exploring the Minecraft source code in 2012. This led me to fixing issues on phones and PCs, sparking my passion for software engineering and computer science.

Currently, I’m a junior at Quinnipiac University studying computer science and artificial intelligence. Over the years, I’ve gained experience with various coding languages like Java, Python, Kotlin, HTML/CSS/JS, and tools such as Git and Android Studio. Some of my projects include a mobile app to explore Quinnipiac University’s campus, a web portfolio with interactive terminal features, and a video game inspired by my campus.

Feel free to explore my work and contact me for any business inquiries or collaborations!
