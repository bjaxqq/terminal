Hi, I'm Brooks!

From a young age, I have had a strong connection and fascination with computers. I vividly remember getting Minecraft in 2012 and delving deep into the files to explore the source code to one of Java's most famous projects. This paired with helping others fix issues with their phones and PCs sparked a lifetime love for software engineering and the world of computer science.

Now in my junior year at Quinnipiac University as a computer science and artificial intelligence student, I have had experience with a variety of coding languages such as Java, HTML/CSS/JS, Kotlin, and Python, and tools like Git and Android Studio. With these tools under my belt, I have been able to create fun and engaging projects including a video game based around my own college campus, a portfolio website with an interactive terminal and API usage, and a mobile app to locate and learn about buildings at Quinnipiac University.
